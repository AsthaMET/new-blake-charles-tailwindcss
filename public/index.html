About Us

In 2017 Blake Charles McCall took over Joseph Cozza Salon. In December of 2019 Blake acquired the Granite Bay location. January 1, 2021 the San Francisco salon was rebranded to Blake Charles Salon. This concluded 25 years of Joseph Cozza Salon. As a leading San Francisco and Granite Bay salon that’s synonymous with luxury hair care, Blake Charles Salon offers a wide range of salon services in San Francisco and Granite Bay. We specialize in luxury hair care and have an outstanding reputation for our high standard of excellence and personalized attention we give to each client we serve. We know that everyone is unique with their own needs, desires and sense of style, which is why we offer a very diversified range of salon services to meet your needs.
